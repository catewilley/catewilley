About Me

Hi, I’m Cate Willey. I am a journalism student at the University of Missouri with an emphasis in reporting and writing.

I am passionate about telling clear, accurate, and engaging stories. I have experience covering community news, sports, and local events.

My background includes reporting for the Columbia Missourian and a sports broadcasting internship with ABC17 News. Through these experiences, I have built strong skills in interviewing, writing, editing, and working on deadline.

Why I Made This Website

I created this portfolio to share my work, highlight my growth as a journalist, and provide a simple place for others to learn more about me.
